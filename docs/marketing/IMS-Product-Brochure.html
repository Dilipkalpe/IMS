IMS Modules & capabilities

End-to-end coverage

Everything your team needs — connected

Sales cycle

  • Sales orders & fulfillment pipeline
  • Customer quotations & price proposals
  • Delivery challans linked to orders
  • GST tax invoices (CGST / SGST / IGST)
  • Sales returns & credit adjustments
  • Customer collections & receipt vouchers
  • Barcode label printing on purchase bills

Procurement

  • Purchase orders to suppliers
  • Goods receipt notes (GRN)
  • Vendor bills & accounts payable
  • Purchase returns & debit notes
  • Payment vouchers & vendor settlements

Manufacturing

  • Bill of Materials (BOM) with revisions
  • Production / work orders
  • Material issue & finished goods receipt
  • Equipment & work-center master
  • Production metrics & output reports

Inventory

  • Real-time stock from all documents
  • Warehouse & godown transfers
  • Opening / closing stock reports
  • Reorder-level & low-stock alerts
  • Stock movement & valuation summary

Finance & compliance

  • Chart of accounts, customers & suppliers
  • Bank, cash & petty cash entries
  • Trial balance, P&L, balance sheet
  • AR/AP aging & outstanding reports
  • Document registers with date filters

Payroll & HR (optional module)

Employee master, attendance, monthly payroll runs, payslips, and workforce analytics — inside the same system.

IMS Deployment & next steps

Technology

Modern architecture. Proven deployment.

Deployment options

  • On-premise — Windows desktop + local MongoDB
  • IIS intranet — IMSWebApp + IMSWebAPI on your server
  • LAN access — teams use browser on office network
  • Database backup — scheduled exit backup & admin tools

Security & administration

  • User roles with granular menu permissions
  • Edit/delete confirmation password
  • Software license management (trial / permanent)
  • Audit-friendly document numbering & registers
  • Configurable themes & company branding

Implementation journey

1. Setup

Install desktop or IIS package, create financial year, company & users.

2. Master data

Products, accounts, warehouses — manual entry or Excel bulk import.

3. Go live

Start with sales/purchase; add production & payroll as you scale.
