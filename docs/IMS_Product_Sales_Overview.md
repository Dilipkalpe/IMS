## Executive summary

**IMS (Inventory Management System)** is a modern, desktop-first business application built for trading, distribution, and light manufacturing operations. It unifies **inventory**, **sales**, **purchase**, **production**, and **finance** in a single, easy-to-learn workspace—backed by a secure **REST API** and **MongoDB** database so your data stays structured, searchable, and ready for growth.

Unlike closed, monolithic packages, IMS is designed for **real-world deployment**: on-premise or cloud database, configurable document numbering, professional tax-invoice printing, and **third-party integration** as a first-class capability—not an afterthought.

## Third-party integration (explicitly included)

IMS is delivered as a **connected product**, not an isolated island. We provide and support integration with established third-party platforms and standards:

| Integration area | Third-party / standard | Business benefit |
|------------------|------------------------|------------------|
| **Database** | **MongoDB** (Community Server, Docker, or **MongoDB Atlas** cloud) | Enterprise-grade storage, backup, replication, and optional cloud hosting |
| **Data access** | **REST API** (Node.js, JSON) | Connect mobile apps, e-commerce, BI tools, custom reports, or partner systems without modifying the desktop app |
| **Database tools** | **MongoDB Compass** and compatible viewers | IT teams can inspect, audit, and export data using familiar tooling |
| **Printing** | **Windows print system** (any installed printer, PDF printers) | Tax invoices and documents on A3/A4/A5 or custom receipt sizes |
| **Deployment** | **Docker Compose** (optional API stack) | Faster rollout in offices and data centers |
| **Desktop platform** | **Microsoft Windows** (.NET 8, WPF) | Native performance on the PCs your staff already use |
| **Future extensions** | Open API surface for **accounting, CRM, e-commerce, barcode hardware, SMS/WhatsApp** | Your integrator—or our team—can wire IMS into your existing toolchain |

**Important for buyers:** Third-party components (MongoDB, Node.js runtime, Windows, printers) are industry-standard; licensing and hosting for those services remain with the vendor you choose (e.g. MongoDB Atlas subscription), while IMS provides the application layer, API, and integration documentation.

We also offer **integration services** (scope-based) for custom connectors, data migration from spreadsheets or legacy ERP, and branded invoice layouts.

## Key features that impress

### 1. Real-time operations dashboard  
Live KPIs for sales orders (open, to ship, shipped, cancelled), products, and finance—so managers see the business **now**, not last month’s spreadsheet.

### 2. Sales order workspace (POS-style)  
- Fast product lookup by **barcode or name**  
- Customer validation from master data  
- **Configurable SO prefix** (e.g. SO, INV, EXP) with **per-prefix numbering** stored in MongoDB  
- **Save**, **Save & Next**, **Save, Print & Next** with professional tax invoice print  
- Multi-tab billing for busy counters  

### 3. Complete purchase cycle  
Purchase orders → GRN → purchase invoice → purchase return, aligned with how distributors actually buy and receive stock.

### 4. Inventory control  
Stock transfer between locations, movement history, warehouse/godown support—stock always traceable.

### 5. Finance & accounting documents  
Payment vouchers, receipt vouchers, credit/debit notes, bank entries, and petty cash—linked to the same product and account masters.

### 6. Rich product & account administration  
Product master with types, main/sub groups, assembly types, sale UOM; account ledger for customers and suppliers; customer types and user management.

### 7. Company registration & branded printing  
Store legal name, GSTIN, address, bank details, and terms once—**every tax invoice** reflects your brand (e.g. retail cloth center layout).

### 8. Settings that stick  
Theme, **A4/A5/A3/custom print formats**, margins, and API URL—saved per workstation in local settings, applied instantly to print and preview.

### 9. Modern desktop experience  
Clean, sectioned navigation; paginated lists; full-page forms (no cramped popups); responsive layout for day-long use.

### 10. API-first architecture for integrators  
Every major entity (products, accounts, sales orders, vouchers, dashboard) is available over **documented REST endpoints**—ideal for:

- Head office reporting in Power BI / Excel  
- Web store order import  
- Partner logistics or accounting packages  
- Custom mobile apps for warehouse staff
