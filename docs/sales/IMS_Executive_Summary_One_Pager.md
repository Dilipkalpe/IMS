### The challenge

Growing trading and manufacturing businesses often run **sales, purchases, stock, and accounts on separate tools**—spreadsheets, legacy software, and manual registers. That leads to stock mismatches, delayed billing, weak collection follow-up, and slow month-end reporting.

### The solution

**IMS** is an integrated desktop application that connects **sales, purchase, inventory, production, and finance** in one system—with a live **dashboard**, document workflows your team already understands (Order → Challan → Invoice; PO → GRN → Purchase Invoice), and reports for management and accounts.

### Who it’s for

| Segment | Fit |
|---------|-----|
| Distributors & traders | Orders, dispatch, tax invoices, multi-godown stock |
| Manufacturers | Raw / WIP / finished goods, work orders, procurement |
| SMEs upgrading from Excel or legacy software | Fast learning curve, Excel import for go-live |

### What you get (high level)

- **Operate:** Sales & purchase documents with status tracking and print-ready invoices  
- **Control:** Stock transfers, opening/closing stock, low-stock visibility on dashboard  
- **Collect:** Outstanding and due aging reports  
- **Decide:** Dashboard KPIs (today’s sales, inventory value, income vs expenses) and MIS / financial reports  

### Why IMS (top 5 points)

1. **All-in-one** — Not five disconnected tools  
2. **Indian business flow** — SO, DC, Invoice; PO, GRN, PI built in  
3. **Built for volume** — Large lists with fast pagination  
4. **Excel import** — Products, accounts, invoices for quick migration  
5. **Desktop speed** — Reliable daily use on Windows PCs  

### Typical results (positioning—customize per client)

- Less time reconciling stock vs billing  
- Faster invoice and dispatch processing  
- Clear view of **who owes you** and **whom you owe**  
- Shorter implementation vs heavy ERP (often **2–6 weeks** with phased rollout)
